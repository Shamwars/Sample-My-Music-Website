Morbid Invasion Records is an independent record label originally based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia in early 2007.Now the office is moved and located in Dublin, Ireland since 2012. The label specializes in extreme metal releases, perhaps leaning more black black metal genre. The label is currently working mostly with the bands from the home country in Malaysia but we are now looking broaden our horizon and will work with any interesting band from all over the world.
